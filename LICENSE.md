Kay Lousberg has put extraordinary care into the KayKit packs, and a great deal of what makes Cozy Lairs feel charming is owed to that work. If you make use of the KayKit Dungeon Remastered pack (for this project, a fork, or any project of your own) please consider:

- **Buying the pack** at <https://kaylousberg.itch.io/kaykit-dungeon-remastered> (pay-what-you-want, with a suggested price). Even a small contribution above the minimum directly supports continued development of new asset packs.
- **Following Kay's work** at <https://www.kaylousberg.com> and on itch.io to discover other packs.
- **Crediting Kay** prominently in any project that uses these assets, even when not strictly required by the license.

Independent creators like Kay are why hobby game development is accessible at all. If the assets bring value to your work, please return some of that value back to them.
